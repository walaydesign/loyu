關於我們

Loyu Int’L Exhibition Service Co., Ltd is an exhibition and tradeshow design company based in Taiwan, established in 1987. The company provides quality service to exhibitors who attend exhibition locally and abroad. We build the stand with octanorm(R8) system, Maxima, carpenter work included.

What we can do for our client?
  1. To help the exhibitor with the documentary.
  2. Check the regulations of fair authority of specific exhibition.
  3. We make design to meet your needs on your budget.
  4. Turn-key service: we offer the carpet, wall partition, furniture, electrical work and cleaning work.
  5. Equipment rental: 65” LED, Touch Screen, iPad..etc.
